WHAT YOUR PASSPORT INCLUDES

The membership
that travels with you.

Privé Passport is the credential that means the game is ready before you arrive. Whether you are spending a week at Marbella Club, passing through London, or heading somewhere new altogether, your membership carries preferred rates, court access, and partner benefits through every part of the network.

At every Padel Destination
  • — Preferred room rate at every partner property
  • — Room upgrade on request
  • — Early check-in / late check-out
  • — Spa, dining, or resort credits where available
  • — Priority court booking
Across the rest of the network
  • — Preferred court pricing at every partner club
  • — First access and early-bird pricing on Padel Experiences
  • — Member access to Privé Locations in Montauk and Coral Gables
  • — Annual membership
