About
The East End's
padel destination.

Privé Padel Montauk is set within Montauk Beach House — one of the East End's most storied hospitality addresses. Opened in 2023, it established the blueprint for everything Privé Padel stands for: refined sport, thoughtful programming, and a venue that draws as much from fine hospitality as it does from the game.

The club runs a full summer season from Memorial Day through Labor Day, with a curated calendar of member events, brand activations, and open play sessions against the backdrop of the Atlantic.

Play at Montauk

How to get
on court.

Three ways to play at Privé Padel Montauk — from full Passport access to single-session bookings.

01
Privé Passport — Full Access
Full Access members ($995/yr) receive unlimited court access at Montauk throughout the season, with priority booking and no additional court fees.
02
Passport Member — Day Booking
Standard and Partner Passport members can book court time at member rates — ahead of public availability with a dedicated booking window.
03
Guest Play
Non-members may book courts as guests of a Passport member, or during designated open-play sessions when available.
