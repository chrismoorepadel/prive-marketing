Montauk, New York

Privé Montauk.

Privé Padel Montauk returns for another season at a new location on Lake Montauk. Launched in 2024, Montauk was where the Privé Padel brand first took shape: two courts, a vision for what refined padel could look like in a luxury travel context, and the earliest version of what would become Privé Passport. The original is back on July 1st.

Privé Miami.
