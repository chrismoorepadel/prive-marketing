Curated Retreats

Destination retreats built
around padel.

Each Privé retreat is built around the destination itself. Padel is at the center — but so is everything that makes the resort exceptional. Whether it's a wine estate in Bordeaux, a jungle sanctuary in the Yucatán, or an island in the Indian Ocean, we draw from the heart of what each place does best. The result is an immersive experience that goes well beyond the court. Invitations are extended to Privé Passport members first.
