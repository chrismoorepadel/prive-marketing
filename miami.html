About
Miami's most
refined court.

Privé Padel at THesis Hotel brings the brand to Coral Gables — a well-located boutique hotel in one of Miami's most distinctive neighborhoods, with a single dedicated court and a programming calendar built around the city.

The club runs year-round — with a calendar that reflects Miami's event-forward culture, its international membership base, and a climate that invites play in every month.

How to get
on court.

Three ways to play at Privé Padel Miami.

01
Privé Passport Members
Passport members receive preferred court pricing and complimentary equipment rental — along with all other Privé member perks at THesis Hotel.
02
THesis Hotel Guests
Guests staying at THesis Hotel receive preferred court rates and waived equipment fees — ask at the front desk to arrange a session.
03
Open to the Public
The court is open to all at standard public pricing — walk-ins welcome, court rentals available. No membership required to play.
