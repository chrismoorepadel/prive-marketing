Padel Club Partners

Where the city
has a court.

In select cities, Privé Padel partners with leading clubs to extend your destination stay. When you're staying at a participating Privé Destination, court access is part of the trip.

LIVE PARTNERS

Three cities. More coming.

LOS ANGELES

Padel Up.

Paired with The Maybourne Beverly Hills and Fairmont Century City.

Six indoor courts in West Hollywood. The first dedicated padel club in Los Angeles.

LONDON

Racketeer.

Paired with The Savoy and St James's Hotel and Club.

Rooftop courts in Soho's Kingly Court. Members-room culture in the heart of central London.

HONG KONG

Go Park.

Paired with Rosewood Hong Kong.

A multi-court facility at Kai Tak Sports Park, Hong Kong's largest sports development.
