SEPTEMBER 2026 · MADAGASCAR

Voaara Madagascar —
Remote Island Escape.

Voaara Madagascar, Île Sainte-Marie

Île Sainte-Marie sits off Madagascar's eastern coast, reached by a private plane transfer from Antananarivo. Days move between courts surrounded by reefs, snorkeling on the north and south reefs, full-day excursions to Îles aux Nattes, and meals shaped by the island and the season.

Seven days · September 2026 · The world's most remote padel courts
FALL 2026 · BORDEAUX, FRANCE

Court & Cellar.

Château de Lussac, Saint-Émilion

Four days at Château de Lussac, an 18th-century estate among the vineyards of Saint-Émilion. Mornings on court with a Privé pro. Afternoons across Saint-Émilion and Pomerol — tastings at properties usually closed to the public, vineyard rides by bicycle, evenings at the long table.

Four days · Fall 2026 · Wine immersion among the Saint-Émilion vineyards
NOVEMBER 2026 · SUMBA, INDONESIA

Nihi Sumba —
Wild Indian Ocean.

Nihi Sumba, Indonesia

Sumba sits south of Bali, reached via a short transfer from Tambolaka. Days move between courts on the wild Indian Ocean coast, bareback horses on Nihiwatu beach as the tide recedes, the Spa Safari treehouse platforms, and the work of the Sumba Foundation in nearby villages.

Five days · November 2026 · Wild Indian Ocean and Sumbanese culture
